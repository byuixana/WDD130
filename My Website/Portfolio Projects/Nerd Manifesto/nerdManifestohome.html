Welcome to Nerd Manifesto

The place to overanalyze your favorite stories

Welcome to Nerd Manifesto, a blog and community where you can overanalyze your favorite stories. Nerd Manifesto was created by Alex Green in 2021 as a website where people can go for a mix of storytelling advice, analysis of the most recent stories, and show how stories shape us. Here you'll find our most recent articles, events being held by the Nerds Manifested community like movie nights, and links to our discord where you can write, read, and nerd out to your heart's content.
