Nerd Manifesto

Nerd Manifesto is a website where members of any fandom can come and discuss the stories they love. There is a home page where users can go to get updates on community activities, see new articles on new stories, and discuss with other fans what may happen next. There is also an article page dedicated entirely to written content where fans can submit their own articles. Finally, there is a page dedicated to a 'Story of the Week' where a new story to broaden perspective is posted weekly.
